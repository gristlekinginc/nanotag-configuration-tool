Report Period must be a multiple of Record Period

Record period - Frequency that the NanoTag records a temperature reading, minimum of 10 seconds
Report period - Frequency that the NanoTag transmits temperature readings, minimum of 30 seconds for US915, AU915, and AS923.  Minimum of 2 minutes for EU868

Use Port 28 for minutes and Port 29 for seconds
Use Base64 value on Helium network
Confirmed downlinks are recommended on Helium network
