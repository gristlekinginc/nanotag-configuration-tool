üìã How to Send Downlink via ChirpStack

  1. Log into your ChirpStack console
  2. Navigate to your application and device
  3. Go to the "Queue" tab
  4. Click "Enqueue downlink"
  5. Set fPort to 28 (minutes) or 29 (seconds)
  6. Paste the generated hex payload (or base64 for Helium)
  7. Set confirmed to true (recommended, especially for Helium)
  8. Click "Enqueue"

üí° Tips:

  • Device acknowledges configuration on fPort 25
  • Report period must be a multiple of record period
